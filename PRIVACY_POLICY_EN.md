# Privacy Policy for "Baby Growth Chart" (My Growing Days)

Last updated: March 09, 2026

Protecting your privacy and your child's data is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how "Baby Growth Chart" (the "App") handles information.

## 1. No Data Collection by the Developer
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from you or your child to our servers. All information you enter remains exclusively on your mobile device.

## 2. Processed Data
The App allows you to store the following data locally:
- Child’s name and birth date
- Gender (to apply growth standards)
- Measurements (weight, height, head circumference)
- Feeding logs and development milestones

## 3. Storage and Security
All data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage.
- **No Cloud Storage**: No synchronization with a cloud or external account takes place.
- **Data Control**: You have full control. Deleting the app or clearing app data in your system settings will permanently remove all stored information.

## 4. App Permissions
The App may request access to the following to enable specific features:
- **Filesystem/Storage**: Required to generate and save reports (e.g., PDF exports) on your device.
- **Sharing**: Allows you to voluntarily send exported data via other apps (e.g., Email). No automatic transmission occurs.

## 5. Third-Party Disclosure
Since we do not collect data, we do not share any data with third parties. No user behavior analysis or third-party advertising SDKs are integrated.

## 6. Your Rights
Since all data is stored locally, you can exercise your rights to access, correction, or deletion at any time by editing or removing entries directly within the App.

## 7. Contact
For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us via the email address listed in the Google Play Store.

---
**Note for Google Play Store**: 
This app is designed for parents but processes data related to children. We strictly adhere to child privacy protection guidelines by ensuring no data is collected or transmitted online.
